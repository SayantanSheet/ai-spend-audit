# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. Problem Statement
Many startup teams and designers overpay for AI tool seats (e.g., ChatGPT Team vs Plus) without realizing cheaper or more efficient alternatives exist. 

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary**: Startup founders & Engineering Managers.
- **Secondary**: Freelance designers and individual developers.

## 3. Channels & Distribution
- **Product Hunt**: Launching as a free tool to capture email leads.
- **X (Twitter)**: Sharing "Audit Results" screenshots with the $ savings highlighted.
- **LinkedIn**: Short case studies on AI cost optimization.

## 4. Viral Loops
- Shareable report URLs (`/audit/[id]`).
- Twitter/OpenGraph meta tags for "Look how much I saved" social proof.

# Product Metrics (KPIs)

## 1. North Star Metric
- **Total Audited Overspend**: Total $ identified as potential savings across all reports.

## 2. Conversion Metrics
- **Form Completion Rate**: % of users who land on home -> complete input.
- **Lead Capture Rate**: % of users who submit email to get the AI summary.

## 3. Viral Metrics
- **Share Rate**: % of audit reports shared on X/LinkedIn.
- **K-Factor**: Average number of new users invited by an existing user.

## 4. Technical Metrics
- **Lighthouse Scores**: Perf >85, A11y >90 across all pages.

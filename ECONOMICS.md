# Project Economics

## 1. Revenue Model (Freemium/Lead Gen)
- **Free**: Direct audit of up to 10 tools.
- **Revenue Lead**: High-overspend users (>$500/mo) are funneled to "Credex Concierge" for manual optimization.

## 2. Unit Economics (Per Report)
- **Compute Cost (Vercel)**: ~$0.001
- **AI Summary (Anthropic API)**: ~$0.01 (Claude 3 Haiku)
- **Email (Resend)**: Free tier (up to 3k/mo)
- **CAC (estimated)**: $0.15 through social distribution.

## 3. LTV Projections
- Conversion to paid audit: 2%
- Average Service Value: $199
- Projected LTV: $3.98
